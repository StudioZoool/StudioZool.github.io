Studio Zoool was founded in 2016 by Muhamad Zulkifie, an aspiring designer + Entrepreneur. The studio provides design consulting, product design, visual communications, packaging design,and prototyping.

Design Philosophy


“The difference between a designer and an artist is the function of a product. When it comes to my approach to design, I like to keep it simple. I work to create useful objects and not just to make things aesthetically pleasing. If a product needs explanation of how it works, it is not that good because things are not as they are but as we are.” - Zul

Awards

Zul has won various international design awards, which includes the International Design Awards and A’Design Award. Currently, Zul is a Designer at Ngee Ann Polytechnic, The Sandbox (An Innovation and Entrepreneurship Office). At Ngee Ann, he works on projects for Ngee Ann, independent projects, design and prototype consulting for students, creating workshops and cultivating a maker culture in Ngee Ann Polytechnic.

Background


Zul’s background includes Architectural Drafting (NITEC), Mechanical Engineering (Higher NITEC), Product Design & Innovation (Diploma), UX/UI Design (General Assembly), Digital Marketing and Product Management.

Zul has 7 years working experience in a private education organization as a Facilities Officer. He left the job to pursue his dream of becoming a Designer. He has 2 years of experience being an entrepreneur by successfully founding an F&B establishment.
